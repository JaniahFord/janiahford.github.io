Janiah Ford: Junior 3D Modeler and Designer

3D Work

A collection of environments, characters, and prop models.
Softwares used include Autodesk Maya, Blender, Arnold, Substance Painter, Zbrush, and Unreal Engine 4.






The Rau Haus (Senior Project) - 2024

This environment was inspired by Feldman Architecture's "Rau Haus" home, constructed in 2023 and located in Portola Valley, US.





The Dock Office - 2021

This exterior was inspired by the work of 3D artist Weitao Sun, found on ArtStation.

The Corporate Office - 2023

This interior was inspired by the typical, everyday modern office, with a bleaker, grayer, almost lifeless aesthetic.






The Grand Suite - 2023

This interior was inspired by Japanese architect Yasushi Horibe's "Grand Suite", a hotel room located on the floating hotel ship "Gunt√ª", that travels along the Seto Inland Sea.





The Bedroom - 2022

This interior was inspired by a simple, modern bedroom, using darker lighting for asethetic purposes.





The Apartment - 2021

This interior was inspired by Geometrix Design's "Axioma Apartment", a futuristic and geometric space and located in Moscow, Russia.



The Spaceship - 2021

This prop model was inspired by Star Wars' X-Wing Starfighter. This was the first ever 3D project I've done that introduced me to modeling.





The Robot - 2023

This character was inspired by the average business man, but instead of doing a regular man model, I wanted to keep everything human except its head/face.
